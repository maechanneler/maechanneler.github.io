Research Interests

Machine Learning & NLP

Specializing in Transformer models, BERT implementations, and patent-specific language models. Developed Japan's first patent domain-specific BERT and implemented various transformer-based systems.

Patent Technology

Research focus on automated reading estimation systems and technological trend analysis using advanced NLP techniques. Developed novel approaches for patent examination support systems.

Fluid Dynamics

Background research in numerical fluid dynamics, MHD generators, and aerospace engineering applications. Particular interest in computational methods for complex flow systems.
